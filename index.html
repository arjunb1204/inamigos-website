From Bilaspur to 28 States — Rewriting India's Story, One Life at a Time.

InAmigos Foundation was born on September 23, 2020, in the heart of Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — not in a boardroom, but in a moment of conviction. Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos began as a simple belief: that compassion, when organized, becomes unstoppable. Today, as a Section 8 registered non-profit licensed by the Central Government of India, we operate across 28 states with six transformative projects that span hunger relief, education, animal welfare, women's empowerment, environmental restoration, and youth skill development.

We are certified under 80G, 12A, CSR-1, registered with NITI Aayog, and hold the IAF ISO 9001:2015 quality management standard — because the communities we serve deserve nothing less than accountability, transparency, and excellence. Every rupee donated is tracked. Every meal served is counted. Every life changed is remembered.

With over 200 dedicated volunteers, 30,000+ trained interns, and a relentless drive to reach the last person in the last mile — InAmigos is not just a foundation. It is a movement. A family of change-makers who prove every single day that India's future is being built not by indifference, but by intention.

Our Six Transformative Projects

Each project is a promise — to feed, to teach, to protect, to empower, to restore, and to build.

Feed Someone Today
🍛

SEVA

No One Sleeps Hungry. No One Faces the Cold Alone.

From railway platforms to remote villages, SEVA has distributed over 50,000 meals and essential clothing to India's most vulnerable — daily wage workers, homeless families, and disaster-affected communities. We believe that dignity begins with a full stomach and a warm blanket.

50,000+ meals & clothing kits distributed
Sponsor a Child's Education

BACHPANSHALA

Every Child Deserves a Classroom — Even Under a Tree.

BACHPANSHALA brings education and digital literacy to children who have been failed by the system — street children, first-generation learners, and kids in underserved communities. Through open-air classrooms, learning kits, and digital workshops, we are rewriting the futures that poverty tried to erase.

Thousands of underprivileged children educated
Protect a Life Today
🐾

JEEV

Because Compassion Doesn't End at the Human Species.

JEEV feeds and cares for 50+ stray animals every single day — dogs, cows, and birds who share our streets but rarely share our kindness. Through daily feeding drives, medical rescues, and community awareness, we are building a more humane India, one bowl at a time.

50+ stray animals fed daily
Empower a Woman
💪

UDAAN

When She Rises, the Whole Nation Rises With Her.

UDAAN empowers women through skill development workshops, menstrual hygiene awareness drives, self-help group collaborations, and livelihood training. From rural Chhattisgarh to urban centres, we are dismantling the barriers that keep women from realizing their full potential — one skill, one conversation, one opportunity at a time.

Thousands of women empowered across India
Plant a Tree With Us

PRAKRITI

We Don't Inherit the Earth. We Plant It Forward.

PRAKRITI has planted over 20,000 saplings across India and championed eco-friendly agricultural practices in farming communities. Through plantation drives, sustainability workshops, and green awareness campaigns, we are growing not just trees — but a generation that breathes responsibility.

20,000+ saplings planted

VIKAS

Skills Today. Careers Tomorrow. Leaders Forever.

VIKAS has trained over 30,000 interns in just four years — equipping young Indians with real-world skills in data analytics, finance, content creation, digital marketing, and social work. Our internship programs don't just build résumés; they build futures, confidence, and a generation ready to lead.

World Water Day 2025 — Every Drop Counts

March 22, 2025

InAmigos volunteers organized water conservation awareness drives across multiple cities, educating communities on rainwater harvesting, reducing water waste, and protecting local water bodies. Hundreds of pledge cards were signed, and saplings were planted near water sources.

On the International Day of Happiness, InAmigos hosted community joy drives — distributing meals, organizing games for underprivileged children, and conducting gratitude workshops. Because happiness isn't a luxury; it's a right.
